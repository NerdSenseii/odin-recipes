Celerita

Ingredients

  • 4 tablespoons chopped celery
  • 1 teaspoon white sugar
  • 3 ounces tequila
  • 1 (12 fluid ounce) can lime-flavored seltzer water

Directions

  1. Muddle celery and sugar together in a mixing glass until crushed.
    Add tequila and stir to dissolve sugar. Strain into 2 ice-filled Collins
    or rocks glasses. Top with seltzer.
